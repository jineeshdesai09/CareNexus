# OPD Management System Status Report - 2026-03-18

## 🩺 1. Doctor (COMPLETED)
**Implemented:**
*   **Prescription Templates:** Saving "common" prescriptions (e.g., "Fever Kit") for one-click entry.
*   **Follow-up Reminders:** Automatically notifying doctors of upcoming follow-ups (Next 7 days).
*   **Vitals Graphing:** A visual trend of patient vitals (Weight & BP) over multiple visits.

## 👤 2. Patient (COMPLETED)
**Implemented:**
*   **Online Booking:** Selecting a doctor, date, and 20-minute time slot for a future visit.
*   **Profile Update:** Ability to change mobile number, emergency contact, occupation, address, pin code, and height.
*   **Billing History:** Viewing billed amounts and receipt numbers within the medical history.

## 🛎️ 3. Receptionist (COMPLETED)
**Implemented:**
*   **Advanced Appointment Booking:** Scheduling appointments for future dates and specific times (e.g. 09:30 AM).
*   **Insurance/TPA Integration:** Capturing insurance provider, policy numbers, and claim status during billing.
*   **Refund Management:** "Cancel & Refund" action in the queue that handles status updates and automated refund remarks on receipts.

## 🏥 4. Admin (COMPLETED)
**Implemented:**
*   **Hospital Profile Editor:** UI to update hospital name, address, and registration fee settings.
*   **Audit Logs:** Comprehensive tracking of administrative actions (User management, Doctor setup, Masters, and System tools).
*   **Backup/Restore UI:** Direct database management (Export SQL dump / Import SQL restore) from the dashboard.
